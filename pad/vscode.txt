config shortcuts
	at first, press the 'ctrl + k' and 'ctrl + s', then you will see the picture of shortcuts configuration. you can search the key you want to change. ~.~

config git
	you should have installed git at local earlier, and press the 'configuration' button (at the left of the screen), search the keyword 'git.path', it`s value may be 'null' or something others, you can write the value to cover that on the user setting part(on the right of screen), such as "git.path": "D:\\a\\git.exe". at last, restart the vscode. now enjoy ~.~
